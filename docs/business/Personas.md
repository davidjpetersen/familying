# Familying.org Personas

## Burned-Out Solo Parent of a Neurodivergent Teen

- **Core tags:** solo parent, neurodivergent child, burned out, phone-only.
- **First needs:** lighten cognitive load, “just-in-time” coping tools, gentle tone.
- **What we serve:** sensory-friendly quick hacks, self-care micro-path, mobile-first UI.

## The Co-Parenting Communicator

- **Core tags:** co-parenting (separate households), co-parent friction, elementary/tween.
- **First needs:** shared routines, de-escalation prompts, legal/logistical checklists.
- **What we serve:** co-parenting tools + calm-communication micro-course.

## New-Baby Rebuilder

- **Core tags:** new baby, exhausted/overwhelmed, limited support.
- **First needs:** sleep/soothing routines, partner check-ins, triage-style onboarding.
- **What we serve:** short, compassionate flow with crisis-first resources.

## Faith-Forward, Structure-First Parent

- **Core tags:** religious/faith-based identity, structure-first values, elementary/teen.
- **First needs:** value-aligned advice without cognitive dissonance.
- **What we serve:** behavior strategies and routines framed in their value language.

## Tech-Strapped Gig-Worker Caregiver

- **Core tags:** gig economy, low-income, mobile-only, anxious.
- **First needs:** budget-friendly, flexible tools; simplified UI; calming supports.
- **What we serve:** flexible meal plans, local aid search, simplified mode.

## Curious, Growth-Ready Planner

- **Core tags:** empowered/curious, likes checklists or deep theory, multi-child stages.
- **First needs:** goal-setting, clear roadmaps, deeper dives.
- **What we serve:** planning templates, expandable “learn more” lanes, age-specific modules.

## Grieving Step-Parent Integrator

- **Core tags:** step-parent, death/loss, isolated, tween/teen.
- **First needs:** empathy first; blended-family guidance; belonging cues.
- **What we serve:** grief-aware prompts, step-family routines, peer stories.
